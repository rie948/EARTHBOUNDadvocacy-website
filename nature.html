Nature & Wildlife

Nature and wildlife form the foundation of Earth’s ecosystems, providing balance, biodiversity, and natural resources essential for life.

This topic is important because wildlife maintains ecological stability, supports food chains, and contributes to cultural and environmental heritage.

Currently, wildlife faces threats such as deforestation, habitat loss, illegal hunting, and climate change, leading to species extinction.

Helping nature requires protecting habitats, supporting conservation efforts, reducing deforestation, and respecting wildlife through responsible human behavior.
