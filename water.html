Water & Marine Life

Water is essential for all life on Earth, supporting ecosystems, human survival, and marine biodiversity. Oceans, rivers, and lakes play a vital role in regulating climate and sustaining food chains.

This topic is important because marine life contributes to oxygen production, food supply, and ecological balance. Healthy waters mean a healthy planet.

Marine ecosystems currently face problems such as plastic pollution, oil spills, overfishing, and ocean acidification caused by climate change.

Protecting water resources involves reducing plastic use, conserving water, supporting clean-up initiatives, and spreading awareness about marine conservation.
